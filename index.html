About Me

Project I’m Proud Of

My most meaningful hands-on project is contributing to our team’s series of winning football matches against rival schools. This required consistent weekly training, coordinating team tactics, supporting less experienced teammates, and staying calm during high-stakes games. This experience is far more than sports success — it’s a masterclass in collective goal-setting, conflict resolution, sustained effort, and motivating a group toward shared victory. The discipline I built here transfers directly to my group STEM work and university research aspirations.
